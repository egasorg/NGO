About EGAS

Founded by young leaders, EGAS designs practical workshops and community projects that meet people where they are. We focus on digital safety, youth empowerment, and inclusive learning.

  • Hands-on trainings in schools and community spaces
  • Open, inclusive, youth-first approach
  • Partnerships with NGOs, schools, and local councils
